The series revolves around two teenage stepbrothers, Drake Parker and Josh Nichols, who live in San Diego, California with Drake's biological mother Audrey, younger sister Megan, and Josh's biological father Walter. Drake is cool and popular, having his own band and being a ladies' man, yet can be absent-minded, while Josh is an intellectual, clumsy and awkward; yet very loving and caring.

Reception:

The series premiere was watched by 3.2 million viewers and also became Nickelodeon's highest-rated series premiere in nearly 10 years.Drake & Josh aired on Nickelodeon and premiered in the United States on January 11, 2004. The final episode aired on September 16, 2007. Reruns aired occasionally on TeenNick from 2008 to 2021.
